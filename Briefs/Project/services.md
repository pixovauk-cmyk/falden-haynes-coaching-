## Entry Point — Free Discovery Call

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| Name | Free 30-minute discovery call |
| Length | 30 minutes |
| Cost | Free |
| Format | Video call |
| Calendly URL | calendly.com/feldeluxe/discovery-call |
| Days available | Wednesday and Thursday |
| Hours | 8am – 6pm |

### What it is
The discovery call is a qualification and trust-building conversation — not a coaching session. Feldon listens, understands the client's situation, assesses fit, and builds rapport.

### What it is NOT
It is not free coaching. It is not an open-ended chat. It has a clear purpose and a clear ending.

### How it ends
The call ends with a verbal one-time offer (OTO): the client's first paid session at the current OTO price, valid for 24 hours. Feldon sends the paid booking link by email within 1 hour of the call ending.

### Current OTO prices by phase
| Phase | Period | OTO price |
|---|---|---|
| Launch | Months 1–4 | £35 |
| Growth | Months 5–8 | £50 |
| Established | Months 9–12 | £75 |

### Copy for the discovery call CTA

Prices rise in three phases as testimonials and reputation build. Each increase is announced 30 days in advance — treat each announcement as a mini-campaign to create an urgency spike.

| Phase | Period | OTO | Standard | 3-session bundle | 6-session bundle |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Launch | Months 1–4 | £35 | £50 | £90 | £160 |
| Growth | Months 5–8 | £50 | £65 | £130 | £230 |
| Established | Months 9–12 | £75 | £90 | £190 | £340 |

### Important note for copy
**Always show the launch price AND the normal price.** The saving creates urgency and frames the launch price as an opportunity, not the standard rate. This is deliberate positioning — it is expert coaching at a fair launch price, not a budget service.
